Features
Everything you need to stay safe
📍

Live Street Heatmap

Every street, lane, and gully color-coded by safety score. Covers all roads including small streets. Updates every hour.

🛣

Real Turn-by-Turn Routes

Actual road-following routes with walking instructions, distance and time — not a straight line. Avoids danger zones.

🚨

SOS Panic Button

One tap sends your GPS location to all saved emergency contacts. Add unlimited contacts from your profile.

📷

Photo-Verified Reports

Every report requires a photo. No fake incidents. Community-verified data you can actually trust.

⏰

Time-Aware Scoring

Same street scores 82 at 6pm and 34 at 11pm. Risk multipliers reflect how danger changes through the day.

🔐

Authority Dashboard

Police and civic bodies see live hotspots, unresolved reports, and auto-escalation alerts for chronic danger zones.

How it works
Four steps to a safer walk
1

Report with photo

Report unsafe conditions with a required photo. GPS auto-fills. Anonymous option available.

2

Score engine runs

Algorithm weighs severity, time of day, report age, and reporter trust — calculates 0–100 every hour.

3

Map updates live

Heatmap updates. Danger zones escalate to police. Safe routes recalculate instantly.

4

Streets recover

Old reports decay over time. Resolved incidents clear the score. Streets improve as they get safer.
